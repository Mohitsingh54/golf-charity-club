01 Project Overview

Subscription-driven golf rewards with charity impact

The platform is a subscription-driven web application combining golf performance tracking, charity fundraising, and a monthly draw-based reward engine. It is designed to feel emotionally engaging and modern while avoiding the aesthetics of a traditional golf website.

Users Will Subscribe to the platform

Users can join on a monthly or yearly subscription and unlock draw participation.

Stableford Scores Enter latest golf scores

Subscribers log their last 5 golf scores in Stableford format, each with a score date.

Charity + Rewards Prize pools and charity support

Users participate in monthly draw-based prize pools and support a charity with part of their subscription.

08 Subscription & Payment System

Commercial and access logic

Plans Monthly plan and yearly plan with discounted annual rate.
Gateway Stripe or equivalent PCI-compliant payment provider.
Access Control Non-subscribers have restricted access to subscriber-only features.
Lifecycle Handles renewals, cancellations, and lapsed subscription states.
Validation Real-time subscription checks on each authenticated request.

09 Score Management System

Input requirements and behavior

Input Requirements
  • Users retain the last 5 golf scores
  • Score range stays between 1 and 45
  • Each score must include a date
Functional Behavior
  • Only the last 5 scores are retained at any time
  • A new score replaces the oldest stored score automatically
  • Scores display in reverse chronological order

10 Prize Pool Logic

Draw and reward system

5 Number Match 40% of pool, full jackpot tier with rollover when unclaimed.
4 Number Match 35% of pool, shared equally across verified winners.
3 Number Match 25% of pool, shared equally across verified winners.
Operational Notes Monthly cadence, admin publishing, optional simulations, and weighted or random logic.
